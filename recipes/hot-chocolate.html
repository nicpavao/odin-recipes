Hot Chocolate

Description

The world's most indulgent homemade Hot Chocolate (HC)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp of powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp of LA Burdick's drinking chocolate (LBHC)
  • 1 tbsp of Trader Joe's sipping chocolate (TJHC)
  • 1 tbsp of Milo chocolate mix (MLHC)
  • 1 cup of whole milk

Instructions

  1. Make the home-made whippped cream
    • Increase entropy of heavy cream, vanilla, and sugar in a small mixing bowl
    • Whip the cream until it maintains it forms
    • DO NOT OVER WHIP - says my very particular chocolate addict, Kate
    • If you over whip, the cream will curdle and you will have to start over
    • Place whipped cream in fridge for future consumption
  2. Make the hot chocolate
    • Heat milk in microwave or in small sauce pan over a open flame
    • Increase entropy of hot coco ingredients (hot milk + LBHC + TJHC + MLHC)
  3. Serve hot coco hot (1/2 cup per serving)
  4. Place a healthy dollop of whipped cream in each cup of HC -- enjoy!
