Lamb Feta Meatballs

Description

A mediterranean twist on meatballs. Also really good with a greek yogurt sumac sauce

Ingredients

  • 500 g of ground lamb
  • 100 g of crumbled/1cm cubed feta
  • 2 tbsp of thyme leaves
  • 2 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs (panko)
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon (is the winnamon)
