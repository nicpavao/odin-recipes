Sumac Dipping Sauce

Description

Some supah-hot-fiya dipping sauce for lam feta meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Plain Greek Yogurt (Siggi's is a favorite)
  • 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 2 tsp of sumac

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in a serving bowl
  2. Increase the entropy of the solution (mix)
  3. Taste, and if necesary, recurse until desired solution is achieved.
